About Us

Hindustan Petroleum (as Esso and Caltex prior to 1974) has been providing aviation refueling services at various airports in India for more than half a century. Today, HPCL provide fueling services to the aviation industry through its business unit, “HP Aviation” also fondly called “Hindustan Aviation”. Its network covers all the major airports in India and is continuously expanding.

Mohali ASF

Mohali ASF is one of the location under aviation fuel distribution network of HP Aviation situated at Chandigarh Internatinal Airport. We had an excellent and dynamic team constantly pushing the boundary of standards towards efficient operational methodology. Tools

ARCMI

Aviation Reports Customized Managment Interface(ARCMI) is the e-ASF tool for storing, viewing and prining of documents/reports used in ASFs. It is our first initiative towards paperless ASF. Developed from scratch at Mohali ASF using open source resources. It is also available under open source for beta version development.
